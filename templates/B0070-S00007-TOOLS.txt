			<title>INTRODUCTION</title>
			<subpara1>
				<title>SCOPE</title>
				<para>This work package lists all common and special tools, supplements, fixtures needed to maintain the Expeditionary TRICON Food Sanitation System.</para>
			</subpara1>
			<subpara1>
				<title>Explanation of Columns in Tool Identification List</title>
				<para>
					<emphasis emph="bold">Column (1) Item No.</emphasis> This number is assigned to entry in list and is referenced in initial setup to identify item (e.g., Extractor (WP 0090, item 32)).</para>
				<para>
					<emphasis emph="bold">Column (2) Item Name.</emphasis> This column lists item by noun nomenclature and other descriptive features (e.g., Gauge, belt tension).</para>
				<para>
					<emphasis emph="bold">Column (3) National Stock Number (NSN).</emphasis> This is National Stock Number (NSN) assigned to item; use it to requisition item.</para>
				<para>
					<emphasis emph="bold">Column (4) Part Number/(CAGEC).</emphasis> Indicates primary number used by manufacturer (individual, company, firm, corporation, or Government activity) which controls design and characteristics of item by means of its engineering drawings, specifications, standards, and inspection requirements to identify an item or range of items. Manufacturer&apos;s Commercial and Government Entity Code (CAGEC) is also included.</para>
				<para>
					<emphasis emph="bold">Column (5) Reference.</emphasis> This column identifies authorizing supply catalog, components list, or RPSTL for items listed in this work package.</para>
			</subpara1>
