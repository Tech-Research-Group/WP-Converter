		<para0>
			<title>INTRODUCTION</title>
			<subpara1>
				<title>Scope</title>
				<para>This work package lists COEI and BII for Expeditionary TRICON Food Sanitation System (ETFSS) to help you inventory items for safe and efficient operation of equipment.</para>
			</subpara1>
			<subpara1>
				<title>General</title>
				<para>COEI and BII information is divided into following lists:</para>
				<para>Components of End Item (COEI). This list is for information purposes only and is authority to requisition replacements. These items are part of ETFSS. As part of end item, these items must be with end item whenever it is issued or transferred between property accounts. Items of COEI are removed and separately packaged for transportation or shipment only when necessary. Illustrations are furnished to help you find and identify items.</para>
				<para>Basic Issue Items (BII). These essential items are required to place ETFSS in operation, operate it, and to do emergency repairs. Although shipped separately packaged, BII must be with ETFSS during operation and when it is transferred between property accounts. Listing these items is your authority to request/requisition them for replacement based on authorization of end item by Table of Organization and Equipment/Modified Table of Organization and Equipment (TOE/MTOE). Illustrations are furnished to help you find and identify items.</para>
			</subpara1>
			<subpara1>
				<title>Explanation of Columns in COEI List and BII List</title>
				<para>Column (1) Item Number. Gives you reference number of item listed.</para>
				<para>Column (2) National Stock Number (NSN) and Illustration. Identifies stock number of item to be used for requisitioning purposes and provides an illustration of item.</para>
				<para>Column (3) Description, Part Number/(CAGEC). Identifies Federal item name (in all capital letters) followed by a minimum description when needed. Stowage location of COEI and BII is also included in this column. Last line below description is CAGEC (Commercial and Government Entity Code) (in parentheses) and part number.</para>
				<para>Column (4) Usable On Code. When applicable, gives you a code if item you need is same for different models of equipment. These codes are identified below:<table id="S0002-10-7360-233-T0001">

				<para>Column (5) U/I. Unit of Issue (U/I) indicates physical measurement or count of item as issued per National Stock Number shown in column (2).</para>
				<para>Column (6) Qty Rqr. Indicates quantity required.</para>
